The Royal Hand-Carved Jesus Panel is one of the most sought-after masterpieces in the Lucas India collection. It is the result of hundreds of hours of precision work by our third-generation artisans, who have dedicated their lives to this noble craft.

Using traditional chisels and gouges passed down through families, every contour of the figure is brought to life directly from the wood — no molds, no machines. The natural grain of the Teak creates a unique texture in each piece, meaning no two panels are identical.

Care Instructions: Dust with a dry soft cloth. Apply a thin coat of teak oil every 6 months to maintain the natural luster. Avoid direct moisture and strong sunlight for extended periods.

Customization: Custom sizes, custom subjects, and personalized inscriptions are available. Contact our design desk for a bespoke quote.
